Administrative App for Committee Members

Features of Admin App

Publish Notices on the Go

Boosts communication efficiency and immediacy within a community by using mobile technology for instant updates and announcements.

Resident, Staff and Services Approvals

This feature streamlines and manages approval processes within a community, ensuring that only verified and suitable individuals or services are granted access or contracts.

Monitor Staff Attendance

With the "Monitor Staff Attendance" feature, organizations can efficiently track employee attendance, ensure precise payroll calculations, and enhance overall operational effectiveness.

Complaint Management Preview

It allows you to get an overview of submitted complaints, track their status, and review resolutions. It helps ensure timely and effective handling of issues, improving customer satisfaction and organizational efficiency.
